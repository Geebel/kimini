TENETS

T.E.A.M

Timeliness

Achieving promptness in all endeavour and pursuits. Respecting timelines, deadlines, and maximising time in meetings while paying attention to punctuality at all times.

Excellence

Achieving effectiveness and efficiency, while being the best at every turn and reaching the zenith of exquisite performance and delivery regardless of cost or price.

Accountability

Achieving honesty and ethical conduct toward others by accepting responsibility for actions. Upholding the highest level of moral behavior and obligation towards others and KIMiN.

Maturity

Achieving constant and consistent change and development in every sphere of life, while adjusting to the lifestyle of Christ and adapting to Kingdom standard.

MEMBERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

  • 1. A Member must head a church or ministry
  • 2. A member must be ready to align with the vision, mission, values and tenet of the organisation.
  • 3. A member must be a bible believing minister
  • 4. A member must have a covering ministry.

mEMBERSHIP RESPONSIBILITIES

  • 1. Commitment to Christ and His kingdom is a paramount responsibility for members
  • 2. Commitment to the vision, mission, values and the tenets Of KIMiN is Essential.
  • 3. Commitment to financial obligation to the organisation is non-negotiable.
  • 4. Commitment to the leadership, goals, project & programs of the organisation is compulsory.

MEMBERSHIP REVOCATION

  • 1. Lifestyle that negates kingdom standard and bring reproach to Christ.
  • 2. Non-challant, abrasive and lackadaisical attitude towards the objectives of the organisation.
  • 3. Dishonoring and disrespecting other members of the organisation.
  • 4. Sabotaging the efforts of the organization.

MEMBERSHIP RIGHTS

  • 1. Right to the organisation's resources, platforms, and covering (optional).
  • 2. Right to protection from the organisation.
  • 3. Right to financial assistance from the organisation.
  • 4. Right to ordination. consecration and inauguration.
